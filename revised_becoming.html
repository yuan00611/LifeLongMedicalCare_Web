Becoming a Patient

We welcome everyone

LifeLong Medical Care believes in helping you get healthy and stay healthy. We believe in giving you the tools you need to live a healthy life. At LifeLong, we care for our community.

LifeLong believes everyone should have access to high-quality health and dental care, regardless of the ability to pay or immigration status.

Becoming a patient at LifeLong simply starts with a call: (510) 981-4100

If you have health insurance

We make it easy for you to become a patient. Call Patient Services and have your insurance information available to make an appointment for the health center of your choice. We accept Medi-Cal, Medicare, most managed care plans, and most insurance plans.

If you are uninsured

We believe in health care for all. Call Patient Services to make a pre-registration appointment. If you are not insured, LifeLong offers uninsured programs and a sliding scale payment option, based upon family size, and income.

One option for health insurance coverage is Covered California.
