Dental Care

Healthy Teeth, Healthy Heart

Our goal is to ensure that all LifeLong patients have healthy smiles. We want to see our patients regularly for cleanings and check-ups. By finding and treating dental issues before they become serious health problems, we help provide a better, healthier life for everyone at every age. At LifeLong, we offer comprehensive dental care for infants, elders, and those in between.

Studies have shown that there is a connection between tooth decay and heart disease. So make sure you take care of your teeth. Brush and floss them two times a day, and see a dentist for regular cleanings and checkups every six months.

LifeLong Dental Care offers services such as:

Pediatric Dentistry

Focusing on the delivery of oral health care to children from infancy throughout their teenage years. A pediatric dentist provides treatment on youth of all ages in various stages of their dentition development. Children can be seen by a pediatric dentist as soon as their first tooth develops.

Adult Dentistry

An essential need for individuals of all ages. It is important that adults seek routine dental treatment every 6 months. Adult dentistry allows patients to maintain their oral health. Routine visits to the dental providerâs office will help prevent oral health issues from arising.

Geriatric Dentistry

Focusing on the prevention, treatment and restoration of the elderly. Patients who are in need of restoration treatment, such as dentures or implants, should contact a dentist that provides specialized care for geriatric patients. Geriatric dentists are specialists in their field and can help resolve specific issues for elders.
